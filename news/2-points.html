Manchester City currently need 2-points to win the league.


By SA STARPLAYER Thursday May-01, 19:20 PM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

Manchester City has extended their unbeaten run of 19 wins away from home. The track record amplified when they beat Crystal Palace 2-0 at the Selhurst Stadium.


Both goals came in within 'a duration of 2 minutes in the second half. Sergio Aguero unsealed the scoreline with a two-touch blitz to strike a volleyball in the 57th minutes. In addition, Ferran Torres came in to score the second goal in the 59th minutes.


With four games in hands, Manchester City can still be crown as 2020/2021 Champions before their next match if Liverpool wins against Manchester United on Sunday.


The Manchester City coach, Pep Guardiola, said he was delighted about their performance against Crystal Palace. "After tough Champions League, it always tough. The players who didn't play in Paris must speak on the grass, on the pitch, and they did that so perfectly well today. In the first half, it was tough, but in the second half, we read them perfectly how to attack them, and after the quality, the players made a difference."


Pep Guardiola said he still feels that they're players who have less quality than Aguero at the dressing room. " What a goal he scored. I'm in love with him as a person. He's an incredible guy. I think they're players who have less quality than him."


Guardiola has also added that it too early for the celebrations. On Tuesday, Manchester City will face PSG in the UEFA Champions League Semi-Final.

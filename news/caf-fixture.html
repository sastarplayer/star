Caf Quarter-Finals fixtures Announced


By SA STARPLAYER Thursday April-30, 1:00 AM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

The draw of the CAF Champions League and Confederations Cup Quarterfinal has been announced on Friday, 30 April.


The announcement was made at the Egypt CAF Headquarters. All teams that finished top in their group will play against teams that finish second in their group.


Caf Champions League Fixtures.


Mamelodi Sundowns (South Africa) VS Al Ahly from (Egypt).


Alger (Algeria) VS Wydad Casablanca (Morroco)


CR Belouizdad (Algeria) VS Esperance de Tunis (Tunisia)


Kaizer Chiefs (South Africa) VS Simba S.C (Tanzania).


Caf Confederations Cup Fixtures


CS Sfaxien (Tunisia) VS JS Kaybie (Algeria)


Orlando Pirates (South Africa) VS Raja Casablanca (Morroco)


yramids FC (Egypt) VS Enyimba FC (Nigeria)


Coton Sport FC (Cameroon) VS ASC Jaraaf (Senegal)


The Caf Champions League President, Dr. Patrice Motsepe, has congratulated all the teams that qualified for the knocko.

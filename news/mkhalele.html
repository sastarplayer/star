Helman Mkhalele: announced as the new assistant coach of Bafana Bafana.


By SA STARPLAYER Friday May-21, 17:30 PM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

Helman Mkhalele has been appointed as the new assistant coach of Bafana Bafana.


The new coach of Bafana Bafana, Hugo Broos, said he was highly impressed by the knowledge of Helman Mkhalele and did not want to hesitate to appoint him as his second assistance.


According to a Media Statement released by SAFA, "Helman Nkosiyethu Mkhalele was born on 20 October 1969, in Newcastle, KwaZulu Natal. During his playing days, he was known as Midnight Express for his tear-away speed that left many defenders in their wake.


He was part of the Orlando Pirates team that won the League title in 1994 and the Caf Champions League in the following year against Asec Mimosa of Ivory Coast."


Right now, Mkhalele was currently coaching the Bafana Bafana team of under 20. The old national team would start their World Cup qualifiers against Zimbabwe on 01 September 2021. The other contenders that are drawn with Bafana Bafana it's Ghana and Ethiopia on Group G.

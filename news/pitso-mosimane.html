Pitso said he's not here for fancy football.


By SA STARPLAYER Thursday May-21, 21:00 PM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

Pitso Mosimane said he had a big responsibility of taking Aly Ahly into the semi-finals of Caf Champions League.


Aly Ahly would face Mamelodi Sundowns in the 2nd leg match of quarterfinals in the Caf Champions League. This game would kick off on Saturday, 22 May 2021, at Lucas Moripe Stadium. Sundowns are coming into this game, trailing back with an aggregate of two goals conceded away in Egypt.


Pitso Mosimane said he has a big responsibility in Egypt. "They are a lot of expectations here. I thought Sundowns is big until I went into the other side and find 70 million people who are supporting Al Ahly. The responsibility is huge. So, what is very important is to try and get the team into the semi-finals and not to outplay or outclass. No, I'm not in that position."


The Jingles said he came into a team that have a lot of achievements


"I came into a team that has 8 stars in the badge, and I made a small contribution to put the 9th star, and the rest came from the supporters and players. Right now, we want to put the 10th star. So, if you come into such a team, you see that it cannot be about me. I'm just a small drop in the ocean." Pitso said.


Sundowns have won once against Al Ahly in their last 8 meetings since the 2018/2019 season, where they beat them 5-0 and repeated with another 2-0 in Egypt. After that, Al Ahly has defeated Sundowns 4 times and drew 3 in the Caf Champions League.

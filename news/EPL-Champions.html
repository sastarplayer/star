Chelsea has won their 2nd UEFA Champions League.


By SA STARPLAYER Saturday May-29, 23:45 PM
  Mdunge Skhumbuzo

Pep Guardiola has Puff his cheeks after losing the UEFA Champions League Final 1-0 to Chelsea in the Do Dragao Stadium.


Man City has never won the UEFA League Final in their entire history. While on the other side, Chelsea has clinched their 2nd UEFA League since, nine years ago from 2012.


In the first 42 minutes, Kai Havertz unsealed a scoreline after procuring a one-on-one chance with Moraes (the Manchester City goalkeeper). In the second half, both teams made an unplanned substitution after losing their key players, Kevin De Bruyne and Thiago Silva, from an injury.


The Man City coach, Pep Guardiola, said they would take this game as a lesson for the future. "To analyze a game, we are sad off-course and congratulation to Chelsea. We have to learn from this, we'll learn for the future, but this was exceptional. I have incredible respect for the players. They did well, they gave everything on the pitch, and unfortunately, we couldn't win, but we'll come back stronger in the future."


With regards to Kevin De Bruyne, Pep Guardiola said they would check on what happened to his eye. "I don't know what happened to his face. I haven't seen him now, I haven't spoken with the doctors, but I hope it won't be a big problem."


The Chelsea coach, Thomas Tuchel, said he was surprised by the starting line-up of Pep Guardiola. " I expected Fernandinho on the starting line-up. But he chose a very offensive line-up, a very technical line-up. It was hard to steer and recover our balls. But everything else, we more or less expected it."


Thomas said they would celebrate to let it sink in that they're champions.

STARPLAYER Youth Development Procedure

We give focus to issues of Infrastructure


South Africa needs football infrastructures at a grassroots level, there are more than a hundred thousand best players who perform from the grassroots level with barriers of infrastructure. SA STAR PLAYER company understands that "THE FUTURE BULL IS AMONGST THE CALVES" so we will allow young players to write to us all soccer-related problems they face in their communities.


Only the coach or community members are allowed to write to us about issues of infrastructure and must ensure to upload their proof of residence before submitting the form, this will help us perform good follow-up of the issue and take it to the corresponding department.

We Develop Soccer Coaching Philosophy to Young Stars.


SA STAR PLAYER will also focus on behavioral, lifestyle, and mental fitness issues of young players. We have noticed that many best locally-based players are failing to succeed due to substance abuse, so we will take queries of all young stars and submit them to soccer experts looking for their advice which possible could motivate and build the player profile. How do we use your donation amount?

Shortage in soccer is huge in the African continent, we use the funding to support our development program by resolving minor and major damages plus other shortages of soccer in the communities, the amount could buy the young footballers equipment such as a whistle, soccer ball, soccer PPE and so forth.
